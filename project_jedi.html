Project Scope

Scope Included

  • Design and engineer a modular satellite Platform optimized for deep space data collection
  • Construct and integrate custom scientific payloads
  • Develop mission software, onboard AI, and embedded systems in-house
  • Perform system integration, functional validation, and calibration of instruments
  • Conduct thermal vacuum testing and environmental simulation
  • Plan orbital path and mission scenarios using proprietary mission planning software
  • Oversee quality control and manufacturing of satellite components
  • Manage payload date formatting and pertransmission analytics

Scope Excluded

  • Rocket Lab will supply and operate the launch site and launch vehicle
  • Ground station infrastructure (satellite dishes, antennas, receivers) will be provided by Rocket Lab
  • Global Robotics will prepare and package the satellite for safe transport but will not manage transport logistics to the launch site
  • Regulatory compliance, permits, and ITU frequency coordination, and orbital licensing will be handled by NZSA and Rocket Lab
  • Cloud storage and backup of satellite data will be maintained by commercial cloud providers AWS Ground station
  • Launch insurance and liability coverage will be arranged by Rocket Lab
  • All media, marketing, or public branding associated with the launch event will be led by Rocket Lab
  • Terrestrial fibre optic data transmission backbone and global data relay systems are not owned or operated by Global Robotics
